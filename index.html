Yego Moravia is a creative director, designer and educator in Los Angeles. He has worked independently or on staff with a number of studios, agencies, brands and publications. Some highlights include Mother New York, Google Creative Lab, and IDEO. He currently works at Stinkdigital. At night, he collaborates with Ariel Roman on HellaCrisis and Mouthfeel Magazine.

Moravia has had the privilege of teaching courses on writing, language, history, and design in San Francisco, Caracas and New York. He taught and developed curriculum at the School of Visual Arts for six years.

Press includes i-D, It's Nice That, Fastco-Create, Fader, Dazed, Mashable, Hardy Amies, Converse, New York Magazine, New York Times and others.
